How It Works

Master the quiz, earn coins, win tournaments – here’s everything you need to know.

🎯 The Basics

  • Goal – Answer 10 questions correctly. You start with 3 lives (hearts). Each wrong answer costs one life.
  • Timer – First question: 14 seconds. Time decreases by 1 second per question, down to 7 seconds. Run out of time = wrong answer (lose a life).
  • Coins – Earn coins by playing free quizzes (1 coin per 2 points) or watching rewarded video ads. Coins are used for lifelines and tournament entry.

🃏 50:50 Lifeline

Stuck on a question? Use the “50:50 Lifeline” to remove two wrong answers. It costs 10 coins per use. You can watch a rewarded ad to earn extra coins if you run low.

🏆 Daily Tournament

  • Entry fee – 50 coins per tournament entry.
  • How it works – Play a quiz in tournament mode. Your final score is submitted to the leaderboard.
  • Prizes – Top 3 players split the prize pool: 80% / 15% / 5%. Prizes are sponsored by telecoms and beverage brands (airtime, data, vouchers).
  • Daily reset – Leaderboard resets every day at midnight (UTC+1). Play often to climb the ranks!

💰 Earning & Spending Coins

  • Play free quiz – Earn 1 coin for every 2 points scored (e.g., 100 points = 50 coins).
  • Watch rewarded ad – Click “Earn Coins” button, watch a short ad, get 25 coins.
  • Spend coins on lifeline – 10 coins per 50:50.
  • Tournament entry – 50 coins per try.

🎨 Categories & Countries

Choose from Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa and 7 categories: History, Geography, Culture, Pop Culture, Movies, Football, and All Africa. Each category contains a large pool of questions (20+ per category) – you’ll never see the same set twice!

📊 Stats & Persistence

Your total points, best score, and coin balance are saved automatically on your device. Come back anytime – your progress won’t be lost.

⚖️ Fair Play & Terms

Questions and answer options are shuffled each game to prevent memorisation. Any form of cheating (multiple accounts, automated play) leads to immediate disqualification. By playing, you agree to our Terms & Conditions.
