How It Works

Master the quiz, earn coins, win tournaments – here's everything you need to know.

The Basics

  • Goal – Answer 10 questions correctly. You start with 3 lives (hearts). Each wrong answer costs one life.
  • Timer – First question: 14 seconds. Time decreases by 1 second per question, down to 7 seconds. Run out of time = wrong answer (lose a life).
  • Coins – Earn coins by playing free quizzes (1 coin per 2 points) or watching rewarded video ads (25 coins per ad). Coins are used for lifelines and tournament entry.

Difficulty Modes

  • Student Mode – 20 seconds per question (min 14s). Best for beginners. 1x coins
  • Scholar Mode – 14 seconds per question (min 10s). The balanced challenge. 2x coins
  • Champion Mode – 10 seconds per question (min 5s). Only for the brave! 3x coins

50:50 Lifeline

Stuck on a question? Use the “50:50 Lifeline” to remove two wrong answers. It costs 10 coins per use. Earn extra coins by playing quizzes or watching rewarded ads.

Daily Tournament

  • Entry fee – 50 coins per tournament entry.
  • How it works – Play a quiz in tournament mode. Your final score is submitted to the leaderboard.
  • Prizes – Top 3 players split the prize pool: 80% / 15% / 5%. Prizes are sponsored by telecoms and beverage brands (airtime, data, vouchers).
  • Daily reset – Leaderboard resets every day at midnight (UTC+1). Play often to climb the ranks!

Earning & Spending Coins

  • Play free quiz – Earn 1 coin for every 2 points scored (e.g., 100 points = 50 coins).
  • Watch rewarded ad – Click “Earn Coins” button, watch a short ad, get 25 coins.
  • Buy coins – Purchase coin packs directly from the shop (₦100 = 50 coins, ₦250 = 150 coins, ₦500 = 350 coins, ₦1,000 = 800 coins).
  • Spend on lifeline – 10 coins per 50:50 use.
  • Tournament entry – 50 coins per try.

Categories & Countries

Choose from Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa and 7 categories: History, Geography, Culture, Pop Culture, Movies, Football, and All Africa. Each category contains a large pool of questions (20+ per category) – you'll never see the same set twice!

Stats & Persistence

Your total points, best score, games played, and coin balance are saved automatically on your device. Come back anytime – your progress won't be lost.

Daily Streak

Play every day to build your streak! 3-day streak = +20 bonus coins. 7-day streak = +50 bonus coins. Don't break the chain!

Quick FAQ

How do I win the tournament?
Score the highest among all participants. Your score is the total points from your 10-question run.
What happens if I run out of coins?
You can earn more by playing free quizzes (1 coin per 2 points) or watching rewarded ads (25 coins per ad).
Can I play offline?
The game requires an internet connection to load questions and display ads. Your progress is saved locally.
How do I claim tournament prizes?
Winners are contacted via the email/phone used during registration. Prizes are credited within 48 hours of the tournament ending.

Fair Play & Terms

Questions and answer options are shuffled each game to prevent memorization. Any form of cheating (multiple accounts, automated play) leads to immediate disqualification. By playing, you agree to our Terms & Conditions.
